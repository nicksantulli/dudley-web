Powell Prowl: Rate Chase Support

A satirical iPhone arcade game about Federal Reserve rate policy

Below are answers to the questions we hear most. Still stuck? Email us with your device and iOS version, the screen you were on, and what happened — a screenshot or recording helps.

Email Support

The game is stuck on a level / something broke.

Tap the gear icon (top-right of the home screen) → Settings → Reset all progress. That clears saved state, unlock progress, and tutorial flags. Force-quit the app and reopen.

How do I unlock the later levels?

Beat each level to unlock the next, all the way to Level 31. The curve gets tight on Hard — drop to Easy in Settings for a more forgiving ride.

My Game Center score isn't showing.

Make sure you're signed into Game Center: iOS Settings → Game Center → sign in with your Apple ID, then open the app → Settings → Leaderboards. If you're not signed in, scores still save locally but won't post publicly.

Can I turn off sound or haptics?

Yes. Gear icon → Settings → Audio & Feel. Sound effects, music, and haptics each toggle independently.

Is this endorsing or attacking a real person?

No. It's a parody with fictional caricatures, in the tradition of satirical comedy. The app makes no political statement or endorsement.

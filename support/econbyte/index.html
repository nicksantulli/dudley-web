Below are answers to the questions we hear most. Still stuck? Email us with your device and iOS version, the screen you were on, and what happened — a screenshot or recording helps.

Email Support

I'm seeing ads — how do I remove them?

Tap the gear icon (Settings) → Remove Ads ($0.99, one-time). After purchase, all ads disappear permanently. If you already purchased, tap Restore Purchases in Settings to reactivate on this device.

My streak reset unexpectedly.

A streak day is credited when you see 3 or more cards in a calendar day. The streak resets if you miss a full calendar day. Check your device date is correct: Settings → General → Date & Time → Set Automatically. If there's a technical issue, contact support at dudleyapps.com/support.

My daily set looks the same as yesterday.

The daily set rotates each calendar day based on cards you haven't seen recently. After completing today's set, tomorrow's set will differ. If you've worked through all 150 cards, the rotation restarts — this is expected.

My bookmarks disappeared.

Bookmarks are stored locally on your device. Reinstalling the app clears local data — there's no account or cloud backup by design. Long-press any card to re-bookmark it.

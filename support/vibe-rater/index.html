Get your vibe rated from a photo — your phone feels the verdict

Below are answers to the questions we hear most. Still stuck? Email us with your device and iOS version, the screen you were on, and what happened — a screenshot or recording helps.

Email Support

Is the rating real?

Vibe Rater is for entertainment. It's a playful, for-fun take on your photo's vibe — not a measurement of anything real about you.

Where do my photos go?

When you pick a photo, it's sent to our rating service only to generate your vibe, then immediately discarded — it's processed in memory and never stored, retained, or tied to you. See the Vibe Rater privacy policy for details.

The phone vibrates while it analyzes — is that a bug?

Nope, that's the joke. It's a Vibe Rater, so it vibrates. You can mute haptics in Settings.

How do I clear my saved results?

Your recent results and saved vibe cards live on your device only. Clear them in the app's Settings, or remove everything by deleting the app.

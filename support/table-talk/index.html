Table Talk: Conversation Cards Support

Shuffle through conversation-starter cards for dinner tables, dates & friends

Below are answers to the questions we hear most. Still stuck? Email us with your device and iOS version, the screen you were on, and what happened — a screenshot or recording helps.

Email Support

I'm seeing ads — how do I remove them?

Tap the gear icon → Remove Ads ($0.99, one-time). After purchase, all ads disappear permanently. If you already purchased, tap Restore Purchases in Settings to reactivate.

My bookmarks disappeared.

Bookmarks are stored locally on your device. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, they won't carry over — there's no account or cloud backup by design. Swipe through your history to re-bookmark the ones you want.

The Daily Featured card is the same as yesterday.

The Daily Featured card rotates once per calendar day based on your device's date. Make sure your device date is set to automatic: Settings → General → Date & Time → Set Automatically.

The card counter isn't resetting / something seems off.

Force-quit Table Talk, then reopen. If the issue persists, go to Settings (gear icon) → Reset Progress — this clears session state without deleting your bookmarks.

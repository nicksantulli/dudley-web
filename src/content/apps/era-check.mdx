## What is Era Check?

Era Check is a free iPhone quiz that tells you which aesthetic era you actually belong to. Answer 10 questions about your preferences, habits, and relationship with being online — and Era Check places you in one of five eras, each with its own visual identity and shareable card.

"2026 is the new 2016" has been everywhere. Era Check is the iOS app that takes that energy seriously.

## The five aesthetic eras

**Tumblr Romantic (2011–2014)** — You've been misunderstood since 2012, and that's kind of the point. Dark plum palette, gothic typography. You find beauty in things that are a little broken.

**Vine Chaos Agent (2014–2017)** — You were chronically online before it was a personality type. Electric orange, chaotic layout. You don't have a vibe — you *are* the vibe, then immediately undercut it.

**VSCO Naturalist (2017–2020)** — You know exactly where the light hits in your apartment. Warm sand tones, film grain. Your camera roll is the most aesthetically consistent thing about you.

**Dark Aesthetic (2019–2022)** — You've always existed somewhere between "I feel everything" and "I'm totally fine." Near-black, deep red accent. Your aesthetic is a mood; your music taste is a personality.

**Quiet Luxury (2022–present)** — You've stopped explaining yourself. Soft oat, minimal. You prefer quality over quantity, silence over noise, and you've developed a very specific reaction to things that feel cheap.

## How Era Check works

1. Answer 10 questions about your aesthetic preferences, online habits, and creative instincts.
2. Era Check scores your answers — without showing you the scoring.
3. An era reveal slides into view with your result, your era's tagline, and a four-line blurb written for you specifically.
4. Tap **Share This Era** to generate a 1080×1080 card in your era's visual style.

The quiz takes about 2 minutes. The result card is designed to look like a post from that aesthetic — not an app screenshot. Post it and ask: "What era are you?"

## The share card

Every era generates its own visual language. Tumblr Romantic is dark plum with gothic serif. Vine Chaos Agent is electric orange with a chaotic layout. Quiet Luxury is soft oat with a single-line frame border.

The card is the point. Users don't post "I got VSCO Naturalist" in plain text — they post the card.

## Free, no sign-up, no camera

Era Check is free to download. It shows one non-personalized ad between the quiz and your result. Remove Ads is available for $0.99 (one-time, no subscription). No account, no camera, no photos — just the quiz and your result.

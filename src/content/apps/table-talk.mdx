## What is Table Talk: Conversation Cards?

Table Talk: Conversation Cards is a free iPhone app of hand-written conversation-starter prompts — 185 of them, across six social categories — designed for dinner tables, first dates, friend groups, and work teams. Swipe through cards, bookmark your favorites, and share them as images to Stories or group chats. No paywall, no accounts, no internet connection required.

## How Table Talk works

1. **Tap a category** — Date Night, Family Dinner, Friend Group, Work Team, Deep Questions, or Light & Fun.
2. **Swipe through cards.** Each card is a full-bleed prompt on a color background. Swipe left for the next, right to go back.
3. **Bookmark the ones that hit.** Heart a card to save it. Open Bookmarks and tap Shuffle My Picks to run your personal deck.
4. **Share or screenshot.** Every prompt generates a 1:1 share image — your card on the category color. Drop it into a group chat, a story, or a screenshot.

The Daily Featured card rotates once per calendar day, determined by your device date — no internet, no server.

## The six categories

- **Date Night** — vulnerability-forward questions that go somewhere. Not "what's your favorite movie." More like "what's something you used to be embarrassed about that you now love about yourself?"
- **Family Dinner** — cross-generational, nostalgic, inviting. Works for everyone at the table, including the grandparents.
- **Friend Group** — playful, slightly roast-able. Zero stakes, high comedy potential.
- **Work Team** — psychological safety builders and icebreakers appropriate for a Monday standup.
- **Deep Questions** — substantive without being therapy. The prompts that actually change the direction of a dinner.
- **Light & Fun** — absurd hypotheticals. Zero-stakes scenarios with no wrong answers and a lot of strong opinions.

## What is Vibe Rater?

VibeRater: Aura is a free iPhone app that rates your vibe from a photo. Pick a selfie, regular photo, squad shot, or pet pic and it scores the image across six dimensions tailored to the mode, then crowns it with a grade, an archetype, and an aura color. Choose a vibe era to re-theme the app and share cards around your palette and voice. And yes: the phone buzzes while it reads you. It's built for entertainment, your photo is rated in memory and never stored, and it's free on the App Store.

## How Vibe Rater works

1. Pick a photo or take a new one.
2. VibeRater auto-detects the mode: selfie, regular photo, squad shot, or pet pic.
3. The app "reads" it — and the phone vibrates while it works.
4. You get a tailored six-dimension scorecard, an overall grade, an aura color, and your archetype.
5. Save a story-ready vibe card, share it, or challenge a friend over iMessage.

The whole loop takes a few seconds and is designed to be screenshot-bait. The archetypes are specific enough to feel personal — getting crowned a "Cozy Goblin" or a "Final Boss" hits differently than a generic "high vibe." In v1.1, your vibe era changes the palette, copy, and share-card style across the app.

## Why Vibe Rater?

Plenty of apps slap a number on your photo. VibeRater is the one where your phone physically responds — the haptic buzz-while-rating is the whole personality. Pair that with personalized vibe eras, mode-aware scorecards, an archetype taxonomy that's actually funny, and share cards built for group chats, and you get something people want to send around. Your photo is rated in memory and never stored, and it's honest about being for fun.

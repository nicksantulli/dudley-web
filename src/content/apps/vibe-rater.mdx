## What is Vibe Rater?

Vibe Rater is a free iPhone app that rates your vibe from a photo. Pick a picture and it scores you across six dimensions — Drip, Aura, Rizz, Main-Character Energy, Fit Cohesion, and Serve — then crowns you with a grade, an archetype, and an aura color. And yes: the phone buzzes while it reads you. It's built for entertainment, your photo is rated in memory and never stored, and it's free on the App Store.

## How Vibe Rater works

1. Pick a photo or take a new one.
2. Vibe Rater "reads" it — and the phone vibrates while it works.
3. You get a six-dimension scorecard, an overall grade, an aura color, and your archetype.
4. Save or share your vibe card.

The whole loop takes a few seconds and is designed to be screenshot-bait. The archetypes are specific enough to feel personal — getting crowned a "Cozy Goblin 🍄" or a "Final Boss 👑" hits differently than a generic "high vibe."

## Why Vibe Rater?

Plenty of apps slap a number on your photo. Vibe Rater is the one where your phone physically responds — the haptic buzz-while-rating is the whole personality. Pair that with an archetype taxonomy that's actually funny and a shareable result card, and you get something people want to send to the group chat. Your photo is rated in memory and never stored, and it's honest about being for fun.

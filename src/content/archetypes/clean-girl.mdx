## What is Clean Girl? ✨

The clean girl aesthetic is a beauty and style look built on the idea that "less, but well-kept" beats "more." It centers healthy, dewy skin, neat slicked-back hair, neutral makeup, and a few simple gold accessories. The result looks unfussy and fresh — like you barely tried, even though the upkeep is real.

## What Clean Girl actually means

Clean girl is minimalism applied to grooming and style. The emphasis is on skin, simplicity, and tidiness rather than bold makeup or busy outfits. The palette is neutral, the lines are clean, and the overall message is "healthy and handled." It overlaps with wellness culture — the "that girl" routine of early mornings and self-care often comes bundled with the look.

From the outside it reads as effortless and elevated. From the inside it's a small set of habits done consistently: skincare, neat hair, a glossy lip, good basics.

## Signs you might be Clean Girl

- Skincare matters more to you than makeup
- Your go-to hair is a slicked bun or sleek ponytail
- Gold hoops or simple studs are basically a uniform
- Your closet is full of neutral, mix-and-match basics
- "Effortless" is the exact effect you're going for
- A glossy lip and groomed brows feel like a finished look

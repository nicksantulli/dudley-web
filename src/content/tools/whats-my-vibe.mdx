<p class="tldr">Your "vibe" is the overall energy, aesthetic, and presence you project — the mix of your style, your social energy, and how you carry yourself. This quiz uses 5 questions to find your closest archetype.</p>

## What's my vibe?

People with a strong, clear vibe are memorable and consistent — their look and their energy reinforce each other. Figuring out yours is mostly about noticing the patterns in how you dress, how you enter a room, and how friends describe you. The quiz below turns those patterns into your closest match in about 60 seconds.

Frequently asked questions

What is a vibe check app?

A vibe check app reads a photo (or your camera feed) and gives you a playful score, aura, or archetype describing your overall energy and aesthetic. They're entertainment apps — fun and shareable rather than scientific.

What is the best vibe check app for iPhone?

For a full vibe scorecard plus an archetype and a haptic twist, Vibe Rater is the most distinctive. For social-photo ratings, Vibe Check – AI Insta Ratings fits; for comedy, Aura Check; for a camera-first game, Aurascope.

Do vibe check apps actually read your aura?

No. Vibe and aura apps are for entertainment — they generate a playful score from a photo, not a real reading of anything. Vibe Rater states this explicitly in the app.

Is Vibe Rater free?

Yes. Vibe Rater is free and ad-supported, processes your photo on your device, and is launching on the App Store soon.

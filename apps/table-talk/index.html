Table Talk: Conversation Cards

Shuffle through conversation-starter cards for dinner tables, dates & friends · Coming soon

What is Table Talk: Conversation Cards?

Table Talk: Conversation Cards is a free iPhone app of hand-written conversation-starter prompts — 185 of them, across six social categories — designed for dinner tables, first dates, friend groups, and work teams. Swipe through cards, bookmark your favorites, and share them as images to Stories or group chats. No paywall, no accounts, no internet connection required.

How Table Talk works

  1. Tap a category — Date Night, Family Dinner, Friend Group, Work Team, Deep Questions, or Light & Fun.
  2. Swipe through cards. Each card is a full-bleed prompt on a color background. Swipe left for the next, right to go back.
  3. Bookmark the ones that hit. Heart a card to save it. Open Bookmarks and tap Shuffle My Picks to run your personal deck.
  4. Share or screenshot. Every prompt generates a 1:1 share image — your card on the category color. Drop it into a group chat, a story, or a screenshot.

The Daily Featured card rotates once per calendar day, determined by your device date — no internet, no server.

The six categories

  • Date Night — vulnerability-forward questions that go somewhere. Not “what’s your favorite movie.” More like “what’s something you used to be embarrassed about that you now love about yourself?”
  • Family Dinner — cross-generational, nostalgic, inviting. Works for everyone at the table, including the grandparents.
  • Friend Group — playful, slightly roast-able. Zero stakes, high comedy potential.
  • Work Team — psychological safety builders and icebreakers appropriate for a Monday standup.
  • Deep Questions — substantive without being therapy. The prompts that actually change the direction of a dinner.
  • Light & Fun — absurd hypotheticals. Zero-stakes scenarios with no wrong answers and a lot of strong opinions.

Why Table Talk?

Every competitor in this category either paywalls prompts after the first three, runs a subscription, or has design that makes you want to put your phone face-down at the table. Table Talk charges nothing, paywalls nothing, and is built to be pulled out on a real table with real people without everyone having to look at a bad app. The conversation about the app IS the conversation starter. That’s the whole trick.

Frequently asked questions

What is Table Talk: Conversation Cards?

Is Table Talk free?

Yes. Table Talk is free to download. The app is supported by non-personalized ads — we don't track you and there's no tracking prompt. If you'd prefer no ads, the Remove Ads purchase is $0.99.

How much is the Remove Ads purchase?

The Remove Ads in-app purchase costs $0.99 (one-time, non-consumable). It removes all ads from Table Talk permanently. You can restore your purchase on any device signed in to the same Apple ID.

What categories does Table Talk have?

Table Talk has six categories: Date Night (vulnerability-forward questions), Family Dinner (cross-generational, nostalgic), Friend Group (playful, slightly roast-able), Work Team (psychological safety builders and icebreakers), Deep Questions (substantive without being therapy), and Light & Fun (absurd hypotheticals, zero stakes). Every category has 30+ hand-written prompts.

Does Table Talk require the internet?

No. All prompts are bundled with the app — no download, no account, no internet connection required. The Daily Featured card rotates offline using the date. Table Talk works everywhere, every time.

What platform is Table Talk on?

Table Talk runs on iPhone (iOS 16.6 or later). It is currently coming soon to the App Store.

Does Table Talk show ads?

The free version shows non-personalized ads between card sessions. We don't track you and there's no tracking prompt. The $0.99 Remove Ads purchase removes all ads permanently.

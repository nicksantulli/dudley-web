VibeRater: Aura

Personalized aura reads, vibe eras, and photo scorecards · Live on the App Store

What is Vibe Rater?

VibeRater: Aura is a free iPhone app that rates your vibe from a photo. Pick a selfie, regular photo, squad shot, or pet pic and it scores the image across six dimensions tailored to the mode, then crowns it with a grade, an archetype, and an aura color. Choose a vibe era to re-theme the app and share cards around your palette and voice. And yes: the phone buzzes while it reads you. It’s built for entertainment, your photo is rated in memory and never stored, and it’s free on the App Store.

How Vibe Rater works

  1. Pick a photo or take a new one.
  2. VibeRater auto-detects the mode: selfie, regular photo, squad shot, or pet pic.
  3. The app “reads” it — and the phone vibrates while it works.
  4. You get a tailored six-dimension scorecard, an overall grade, an aura color, and your archetype.
  5. Save a story-ready vibe card, share it, or challenge a friend over iMessage.

The whole loop takes a few seconds and is designed to be screenshot-bait. The archetypes are specific enough to feel personal — getting crowned a “Cozy Goblin” or a “Final Boss” hits differently than a generic “high vibe.” In v1.1, your vibe era changes the palette, copy, and share-card style across the app.

Why Vibe Rater?

Plenty of apps slap a number on your photo. VibeRater is the one where your phone physically responds — the haptic buzz-while-rating is the whole personality. Pair that with personalized vibe eras, mode-aware scorecards, an archetype taxonomy that’s actually funny, and share cards built for group chats, and you get something people want to send around. Your photo is rated in memory and never stored, and it’s honest about being for fun.

Frequently asked questions

What is Vibe Rater?

VibeRater: Aura is a free iPhone entertainment app that rates your vibe from a photo. It scores selfies, regular photos, squad shots, and pet pics across six dimensions, then assigns an archetype, an aura color, and a shareable vibe card. The phone vibrates while it reads you.

How does Vibe Rater rate your vibe?

You pick or take a photo, and VibeRater analyzes it to produce a playful score across six vibe dimensions, plus an overall grade, archetype, aura color, and share card. In v1.1, it auto-detects selfie, regular photo, squad, and pet modes so the scorecard matches what you uploaded. It's designed to be fun and shareable, not a real measurement of anything.

Is Vibe Rater accurate?

Vibe Rater is for entertainment only. Its scores, grades, and archetypes are meant to be funny and shareable — not a measurement of anything real about you or anyone in a photo.

Does Vibe Rater upload my photos?

Your photo is sent to our rating service only to generate your vibe, then immediately discarded — it's processed in memory and never stored, retained, or tied to you. We don't use it for tracking or ads. See the Vibe Rater privacy policy for details.

Is Vibe Rater free?

Yes. VibeRater: Aura is free to download on the App Store. Ads keep it free, and a one-time Remove Ads purchase is available for $0.99.

What archetypes does Vibe Rater have?

VibeRater assigns archetypes like Main Character Energy, Final Boss, Quiet Luxury, Clean Girl, Dark Academia, Cottagecore, Cozy Goblin, Gorpcore Prophet, NPC Affectionate, and more — based on your dimension scores and photo mode.

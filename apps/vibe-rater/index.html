Vibe Rater

Get your vibe rated from a photo — your phone feels the verdict · Coming soon

What is Vibe Rater?

Vibe Rater is a free iPhone app that rates your vibe from a photo. Pick a picture and it scores you across six dimensions — Drip, Aura, Rizz, Main-Character Energy, Fit Cohesion, and Serve — then crowns you with a grade, an archetype, and an aura color. And yes: the phone buzzes while it reads you. It’s built for entertainment, your photo is rated in memory and never stored, and it’s free on the App Store.

How Vibe Rater works

  1. Pick a photo or take a new one.
  2. Vibe Rater “reads” it — and the phone vibrates while it works.
  3. You get a six-dimension scorecard, an overall grade, an aura color, and your archetype.
  4. Save or share your vibe card.

The whole loop takes a few seconds and is designed to be screenshot-bait. The archetypes are specific enough to feel personal — getting crowned a “Cozy Goblin 🍄” or a “Final Boss 👑” hits differently than a generic “high vibe.”

Why Vibe Rater?

Plenty of apps slap a number on your photo. Vibe Rater is the one where your phone physically responds — the haptic buzz-while-rating is the whole personality. Pair that with an archetype taxonomy that’s actually funny and a shareable result card, and you get something people want to send to the group chat. Your photo is rated in memory and never stored, and it’s honest about being for fun.

Frequently asked questions

What is Vibe Rater?

Vibe Rater is a free iPhone entertainment app that rates your vibe from a photo. It scores you across six dimensions — Drip, Aura, Rizz, Main-Character Energy, Fit Cohesion, and Serve — then assigns an archetype, an aura color, and a shareable vibe card. The phone vibrates while it reads you.

How does Vibe Rater rate your vibe?

You pick or take a photo, and Vibe Rater analyzes it to produce a playful score across six vibe dimensions, plus an overall grade and archetype. It's designed to be fun and shareable, not a real measurement of anything.

Is Vibe Rater accurate?

Vibe Rater is for entertainment only. Its scores, grades, and archetypes are meant to be funny and shareable — not a measurement of anything real about you or anyone in a photo.

Does Vibe Rater upload my photos?

Your photo is sent to our rating service only to generate your vibe, then immediately discarded — it's processed in memory and never stored, retained, or tied to you. We don't use it for tracking or ads. See the Vibe Rater privacy policy for details.

Is Vibe Rater free?

Yes. Vibe Rater is free to download on the App Store. The app is supported by non-personalized ads — we don't track you and there's no tracking prompt.

What archetypes does Vibe Rater have?

Vibe Rater assigns archetypes like Main Character Energy, Final Boss, Quiet Luxury, Clean Girl, Dark Academia, Cottagecore, Cozy Goblin, and more — based on your dimension scores.

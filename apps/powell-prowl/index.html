Powell Prowl: Rate Chase

A satirical iPhone arcade game about Federal Reserve rate policy · Live on the App Store

What is Powell Prowl: Rate Chase?

Powell Prowl: Rate Chase is a free satirical arcade game for iPhone — a fast, fair, 31-level romp where you play as a hapless President figure chasing the Fed Chair around a paper-cutout Washington. Catch him, hand over your rate-cut memo, rack up points, and climb the Game Center leaderboards, all with a sense of humor about the whole spectacle. It’s free on the App Store with no in-app purchases.

How Powell Prowl works

Each level drops you into a quick arcade challenge with tight, responsive controls. You chase the Fed Chair down — tagging him, sniping subpoenas, arm-wrestling, racing canoes, whacking Fed officials and more — against the clock, beat the level, and unlock the next one, all the way to Level 31. The difficulty curve ramps up steadily; switch to Easy in Settings for a gentler ride, or Hard if you want the curve to bite.

Sessions are short and pick-up-and-play. Sound effects, music, and haptics each toggle independently, and your best scores post to Game Center leaderboards when you’re signed in.

Why Powell Prowl?

Most apps about economics take themselves very seriously. Powell Prowl does the opposite: it turns rate-setting theater into a tight little arcade game with real polish — responsive controls, satisfying haptics, and a parody it commits to fully. If you’ve ever rolled your eyes at a Fed press conference, this is the game that lets you laugh at it.

Frequently asked questions

What is Powell Prowl: Rate Chase?

What iOS version does Powell Prowl require?

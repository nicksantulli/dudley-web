Frequently asked questions

How accurate is this vibe quiz?

This quiz is for entertainment — it's based on 5 style and energy questions and is meant to be fun, not scientific. For a photo-based vibe read with a full dimension score, download Vibe Rater.

What is a vibe archetype?

A vibe archetype is a label for a distinct cluster of aesthetic, energy, and style choices — like Main Character Energy, Quiet Luxury, or Cottagecore. Archetypes are shorthand for a whole constellation of traits that tend to travel together.

How do I get my vibe rated from a photo?

Download Vibe Rater on the App Store for iPhone. Pick or take a photo and the app rates your vibe across six dimensions and assigns your archetype — and the phone vibrates while it reads you.

What's the difference between a vibe and an aesthetic?

An aesthetic is primarily visual — what your outfits and spaces look like. A vibe includes aesthetic but also energy, presence, and social dynamics. You can have quiet luxury aesthetics but main character energy, for example.

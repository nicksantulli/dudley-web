Rizz

Natural charisma and the ability to charm anyone

Rizz is slang for charisma — the natural ability to charm, flirt with, or attract people through confidence, style, and conversation.

What is Rizz? 😎

Rizz is short for “charisma,” and it describes someone’s ability to attract or charm others — often in a romantic or flirtatious context, but really in any social setting. If someone “has rizz,” they’re smooth, confident, and easy to be drawn to. The term blew up online and went fully mainstream, even landing as a word of the year in 2023.

What Rizz actually means

Rizz is charm in action. It shows up as effortless conversation, good timing, confident body language, and the ability to make other people feel at ease (or a little dazzled). There are tiers to it: regular rizz is verbal charm, while “unspoken rizz” is the rarer skill of attracting people without saying anything at all — pure presence.

From the outside, rizz looks like someone who never seems nervous and always knows what to say. From the inside, it’s usually a mix of genuine confidence, attentiveness, and not taking the moment too seriously.

Signs you might have Rizz

  • Conversations with strangers come easy to you
  • You can read a room and match its energy instantly
  • People remember meeting you
  • You’re comfortable with a little eye contact and a slow smile
  • Banter is your natural setting
  • You’ve made someone laugh within 30 seconds of meeting them

Frequently asked questions

What does rizz mean?

Rizz is slang for charisma — someone's ability to charm, flirt, or attract others through style, confidence, and conversation. Having 'rizz' means you're naturally smooth and magnetic in social or romantic situations.

Where does the word rizz come from?

Rizz is shortened from 'charisma.' It was popularized online around 2021–2022 and became mainstream enough to be named a word of the year in 2023. The verb form is 'to rizz someone up.'

What is 'unspoken rizz'?

Unspoken rizz refers to charm that works without any words — attracting people through presence, body language, eye contact, and confidence alone. It's considered the highest tier of rizz.

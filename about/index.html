About Dudley Development

Independent iOS studio · United States

Dudley Development is a small independent studio making iPhone apps — the kind that are quick to pick up, do one thing with a bit of personality, and don't ask anything of you to start playing.

What we believe

  • Free to start. Every app is free to download and play. No paywalls in the way of the fun.
  • No accounts. You don't sign up, log in, or hand over an email to use a Dudley app.
  • Your data stays yours. We don't run our own analytics or trackers. What an app saves, it saves on your device.
  • Honest about ads. Our free apps are ad-supported through Google AdMob, disclosed plainly, and you can always decline tracking.
  • Polish is the point. Tight controls, real haptics, and a sense of humor — details that make an app feel made, not generated.

The apps

Powell Prowl: Rate Chase — a 31-level arcade satire, live on the App Store. Vibe Rater — a tongue-in-cheek photo-vibe rater that buzzes while it reads you, currently in development. See them both on the home page.

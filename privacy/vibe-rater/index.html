Privacy Policy

Vibe Rater · Last updated June 14, 2026

The short version

Vibe Rater is a free, for-entertainment app supported by ads. When you pick or take a photo, the app sends it to our service to generate your playful "vibe" rating, then the photo is discarded — it is processed in memory and never stored or retained. We don't run accounts, profiles, or analytics of our own. To show ads, the app uses Google AdMob, as described below — and you can say no to tracking with one tap.

How your photo is used

When you pick or take a photo, Vibe Rater sends it to our rating service, which produces the playful "vibe" score and verdict. Your photo is used only to generate that rating, processed in memory, and is never stored, retained, logged, or shared — it isn't saved on our servers and isn't tied to your name, account, or any identifier. The app requests photo-library or camera access only so you can choose the image you want rated.

We don't sell your photos or use them to build profiles or to train models. The rating is generated and the image is dropped.

What stays on your device

  • Your recent results and any saved vibe cards (stored locally so you can revisit them).
  • Your sound and haptics preferences.

This is stored on your device. Uninstalling the app, or clearing history in Settings, removes it.

For entertainment only

Vibe Rater is a novelty. Its scores, grades, and archetypes are meant to be fun and are not a measurement of anything real about you, your appearance, or any person in a photo.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app shows occasional full-screen ads between ratings. Ads are served by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising network. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect device information such as the advertising identifier (IDFA), IP address, and coarse ad-interaction data. We do not receive, store, or link any of this to you.

  • Your choice: the first time an ad is about to appear, iOS asks whether to allow tracking. If you choose Ask App Not to Track, you still get ads — just non-personalized ones.
  • Change your mind anytime: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → Vibe Rater.
  • How Google uses this data is described in Google's advertising policy and Google's privacy policy.

Children

We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. There are no accounts, profiles, or in-app messaging, and the app is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes materially, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date above. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance.
